Tânia Raposo is a designer, teacher and researcher. Currently living in France working on a PhD with the University of Paris VIII in collaboration with the ANRT, and teaching typography at the Type and Media Master, KABK and at ENSAD Nancy.

She worked as the Educational Program Coordinator and curatorial assistant at Letterform Archive, she taught at the California College of the Arts, San Francisco City College and Type@Cooper West program. Previously she worked at the graphic design studios Atlas in Mallorca and itemzero and the type foundry Feliciano Type in Portugal.

You can find her on Twitter and Instagram, where she she shares her books and analogue photography. You can discover her Guide Book collection. Read some of her articles on Alphabettes. Or send her an email.
